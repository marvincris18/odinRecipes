Bulalo

Home

Experience the ultimate comfort of Bulalo, a deeply satisfying Filipino beef bone marrow soup. This hearty dish features tender beef shanks simmered for hours, releasing rich
flavors into a clear, savory broth. The addition of corn, cabbage, and pechay adds a touch of sweetness and freshness, making this a complete and nourishing meal. Perfect for cool
evenings or family gatherings, Bulalo is a true Filipino culinary treasure.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef shanks with bone marrow
  • 1 large onion, quartered
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 thumb-sized ginger, sliced
  • Fish sauce (patis) to taste
  • Whole peppercorns
  • Water
  • 2 corn on the cob, cut into pieces
  • 1 cabbage, quartered
  • Several stalks of pechay (bok choy)
  • Green onions, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. In a large pot, place the beef shanks, onion, garlic, ginger, fish sauce, and whole peppercorns.
  2. Add enough water to cover the beef. Bring to a boil.
  3. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 2-3 hours, or until the beef is very tender and the bone marrow is cooked.
  4. Add the corn on the cob and cook for about 10 minutes.
  5. Add the cabbage and pechay and cook until wilted.
  6. Season with more fish sauce and pepper to taste, if needed.
  7. Garnish with chopped green onions before serving.
  8. Serve hot with rice.
