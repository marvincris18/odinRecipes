Caldereta

Home

Dive into the rich and savory world of Caldereta, a beloved Filipino stew that's perfect for family gatherings. Tender beef cubes are simmered in a flavorful tomato-based sauce, enriched with liver spread and peanut butter, creating a truly unique and satisfying dish. Loaded with vegetables like potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers, this Caldereta is a hearty and comforting meal

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef chuck, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 (8 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (12 oz) can tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1/2 cup liver spread
  • 1/4 cup peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 potatoes, cubed
  • 2 carrots, cubed
  • 1 cup green peas
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Bay leaves
  • Water

Steps

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  2. Brown the beef cubes on all sides. Remove from the pot and set aside.
  3. Saut√© the onion and garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add the bell peppers and cook until slightly tender.
  5. Stir in the tomato sauce, tomato paste, beef broth, liver spread, peanut butter, and soy sauce. Mix well.
  6. Return the beef to the pot. Add bay leaves.
  7. Add enough water to cover the beef. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and simmer for 1-1.5 hours, or until the beef is tender.
  8. Add the potatoes and carrots and cook until tender.
  9. Add the green peas and cook for a few more minutes.
  10. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  11. Serve hot with rice.
