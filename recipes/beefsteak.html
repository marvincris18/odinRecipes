Beef Steak

Home

This classic Filipino Beefsteak recipe delivers tender slices of beef,
marinated in a tangy soy-calamansi blend, then cooked with caramelized
onions and bell peppers. Simple to make, and bursting with flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef sirloin or tenderloin, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice or calamansi juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, sliced into rings
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced (optional)
  • 1/4 cup water or beef broth

Steps

  1. Marinate the beef slices in soy sauce, lemon juice (or calamansi juice), and ground black pepper for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Saut√© the garlic until golden brown. Add the onion rings and cook until softened.
  4. Add the marinated beef slices and cook until browned on all sides.
  5. Add the bell peppers and cook for a few minutes until slightly tender.
  6. Pour in the water or beef broth and bring to a simmer. Cook until the sauce has thickened slightly.
  7. Serve hot with rice.
