Halo-halo

Home

Dive into the delightful world of Halo-halo, the ultimate Filipino summer treat! This vibrant dessert is a medley of sweet ingredients, layered with shaved ice and drizzled with evaporated milk. From sweetened beans and jackfruit to ube halaya and leche flan, each spoonful is a burst of flavor and texture. Top it off with a scoop of ube ice cream gitand your favorite optional toppings for a truly indulgent experience.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shaved ice
  • 1/4 cup evaporated milk
  • 2 tablespoons sweetened beans (red beans, white beans, or garbanzos)
  • 2 tablespoons sweetened jackfruit
  • 2 tablespoons nata de coco (coconut gel)
  • 2 tablespoons macapuno strings (coconut sport)
  • 2 tablespoons ube halaya (purple yam jam)
  • 2 tablespoons leche flan
  • 1 scoop ube ice cream
  • Optional toppings: pinipig (toasted rice flakes), additional fruits (mango, banana), small tapioca pearls, sweet corn, gulaman (jelly).

Steps

  1. In a tall glass, layer the sweetened beans, jackfruit, nata de coco, and macapuno strings.
  2. Fill the glass with shaved ice.
  3. Pour evaporated milk over the shaved ice.
  4. Top with ube halaya, leche flan, and a scoop of ube ice cream.
  5. Add any optional toppings, such as pinipig or additional fruits.
  6. Mix well before eating.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
